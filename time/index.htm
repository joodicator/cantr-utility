This page converts between lengths of time expressed in Cantr form (used in-game) and those expressed in Earth form (used out-of-game).

Days Hours Minutes Seconds
Earth form

It is based on the following facts:

1 Cantr day = 8 Cantr hours = 1 Earth day
1 Cantr hour = 36 Cantr minutes = 3 Earth hours
1 Cantr minute = 60 Cantr seconds = 5 Earth minutes
1 Cantr second = 5 Earth seconds
